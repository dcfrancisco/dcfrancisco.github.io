Chronicle

Overview

Chronicle is an AI biographer — a system that captures memories, artifacts, and conversations to build a narrative that can be explored, questioned, and exported.

Motivation

Chronicle asks: how do we capture personal memory in a way that preserves provenance and remains useful over time?

Personal history is scattered across formats and systems. Chronicle aims to assemble that evidence into a coherent, verifiable story that can be preserved and shared.

Approach

  • Capture structured and unstructured artifacts.
  • Index memories with provenance and timestamps.
  • Generate narratives that link to source evidence rather than opaque summaries.

Privacy & export

Designed as privacy-first: data can be exported in readable formats, shared selectively, or stored offline under user control.
