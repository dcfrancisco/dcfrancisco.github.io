EventEase

Event management platform covering ticketing, scheduling, and back office administration.

Overview

EventEase is a practical event management platform built to remove friction for organizers. It focuses on ticketing, scheduling, and reliable back-office workflows.

Approach

  • Simple, robust APIs for ticketing and scheduling.
  • Integrations with payments and calendar systems.
  • Operational dashboards for event operations and reconciliation.

Architecture highlights

Built with reliability and observability in mind — event flows are modeled as auditable processes with clear retry and compensation strategies.
