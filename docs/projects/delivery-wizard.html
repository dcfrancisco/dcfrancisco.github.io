Delivery Wizard

Overview

Delivery Wizard provides an intelligence layer that helps teams validate and govern software delivery. It gathers evidence, scores risk, and surfaces issues before they reach production.

Motivation

Delivery and compliance are often manual and brittle. By codifying standards and automatically collecting evidence, Delivery Wizard reduces reviewer burden and increases confidence in releases.

Approach & features

  • Connects to CI/CD to collect artifacts and test outputs.
  • Runs validation rules and ML checks against delivery artifacts.
  • Produces risk scores and a traceable evidence package for reviewers.

Architecture highlights

Designed as an orchestrator that integrates with pipelines, knowledge indices, and governance policies. It emits review artifacts and supports human override where necessary.
