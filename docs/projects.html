Projects

Work organized around ideas I follow: memory, governance, platform composition, and practical AI. Each project is an experiment in how systems preserve knowledge and help people make better decisions.

Open Intelligence Platform (OIP)

Narrative: Build a platform where memory, model routing, and governance are first-class. OIP experiments with pluggable model backends, vector memory for retrieval, and audit trails that tie outputs back to evidence.

Delivery Wizard

Narrative: Reduce delivery risk by codifying standards and collecting evidence. Delivery Wizard connects CI pipelines, knowledge indices, and validation rules to create an automated review layer for teams and auditors.

Chronicle

Narrative: Treat personal history as structured memory. Chronicle uses conversations and source artifacts to create narratives that can be questioned, verified, and exported for legacy purposes.

Athena

Narrative: Support learning with assessment and retrieval. Athena focuses on retention, adaptive practice, and analytics to guide study and measure progress over time.

EventEase

Narrative: Practical business software that removes friction from event administration — ticketing, scheduling, and integrations with payment and calendar systems.
